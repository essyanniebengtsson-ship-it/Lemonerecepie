creamy chicken stew with Lemon and mascarpone

    ingredients

  • 4st chicken fillets

  • 2 finely chopped garlic cloves

  • 1 Lemon

  • 2dl chicken stock (2dl water + 1tsp concentrated broth)

  • 1 packaging marcarpone

  • salt

  • blackpepper

  1. split the chicken on the middle and fry the chicken fillets until golden brown in olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Remove the chicken and place on a plate.
  3. Grate the lemon zest and squeeze the juice from the lemon, set aside.
  4. Fry the garlic in the remaining fat in the pan until soft. Add the lemon juice, lemon zest and chicken stock and bring to a boil.
  5. Add the mascarpone and simmer until a creamy sauce forms. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  6. Add the chicken fillets and cook until cooked through.
  7. Place on plates, grate Parmesan and chopped parsley over.
  8. Serve with freshly cooked pasta or rice.
