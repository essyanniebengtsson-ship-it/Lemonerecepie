Zeinas pasta limone

    ingredienser

  • 400g spagetti

  • 3 Lemon

  • 5dl whipped cream

  • 50g butter

  • 1dl finely grated parmesan

  • 1krm salt

  • 1krm black pepper

  • 1mtsk parsley (for garnish)

  1. Cook the pasta al dente in salted water. Drain the water but save 1.5 dl of the pasta cooking water. Rinse the pasta in cold water.
  2. Wash the lemons and grate the zest (only the yellow part) with a zester. Mix the lemon zest and cream in a pot or frying pan with high sides and bring the sauce to the boil, reduce the heat and simmer slowly for about 5-6 minutes.
  3. Chop the butter into large cubes and add one cube at a time to the sauce while whisking. Fold in the cheese and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss the pasta into the sauce and if the sauce feels too dry, gently thin it with a little of the pasta water.
  5. Garnish with finely chopped parsley and serve directly!
  6. If you want even more lemon flavor in the sauce, you can squeeze lemon juice over it when serving.
