Homemade pasta

    ingredients

  • 5dl durum wheat

  • 4st eggs

  1. Measure the flour onto a clean surface.
  2. make a well in the middle of the flour
  3. hatch the eggs in the hole
  4. Knead together into a dough, if it becomes a little dry you can add water a little at a time, until you have a soft, pliable dough.
  5. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and let it rest in the fridge for an hour.
  6. slice the dough into 4 parts
  7. Roll the dough thinly or run the dough through the pasta machine several times at different levels to get an elastic thin pasta.
  8. nu can shape your pasta however you like
  9. Now let your pasta dry or boil the pasta directly for a few minutes and enjoy your homemade pasta.
